About 

Adarsha Mitra Mandali ( AMM) is a non profit charitable organization founded in November 2012 by like minded friends who came together to create a healthy society to improve the lives of all children by providing basic fundamental rights.
Objectives include helping schools, children and communities in and around Adoni

Primary Goals

Providing Basic School Necessities

School Uniforms, Books, Notebooks, School bags, Stationary etc
Classroom Equipment, Furniture Teaching and learning materials 

Support poor secondary and primary school students financially

Financial Educational Support

Provide career guidance and support education initiatives.

Students confidence building workshops and seminars 

Provide and support self employment initiatives  

Sewing machines for women ( as a tool for self employment)
